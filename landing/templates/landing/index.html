Who are CFO Insights?

The who, the why, the what.

CFO Insights was founded on the idea that a business is nothing more than a collection of people. We might use technology in our day to day work and the collection of people might be united behind a company name, but it is the people and the decisions they make that define the future of any company.

That's why we believe in the importance of bringing business leaders together in a neutral forum where they can learn and grow together. Otto von Bismarck famously said “Fools learn from experience. I prefer to learn from the experience of others.” and that is the core reason for why CFO Insights exists - so that we may learn from each other.
